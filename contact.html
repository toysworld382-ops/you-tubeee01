Quick Help - Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my download failing? +

Download failures can occur due to several reasons:

  • Network issues: Check your internet connection
  • Video restrictions: Some videos may be private or region-locked
  • Quality selection: Try a different quality option
  • Server load: Try again in a few minutes

If the problem persists, please contact our support team with the video URL.

Is this service free? +

Yes! Our YouTube downloader is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium accounts required. We believe everyone should have access to download their favorite videos.

What video qualities are supported? +

We support multiple quality options:

  • 4K (2160p): Highest available quality
  • Full HD (1080p): High quality for most uses
  • HD (720p): Good quality, smaller file size
  • SD (480p): Lower quality, fastest download
  • Audio Only: Extract audio in M4A format
Is it legal to download YouTube videos? +

The legality depends on several factors:

  • Personal use: Generally acceptable for personal, non-commercial use
  • Copyright: You must have permission to download copyrighted content
  • Fair use: Educational use may be covered under fair use provisions
  • Your content: You can always download your own uploaded videos

Please ensure you comply with copyright laws and YouTube's Terms of Service.

Do you store my downloaded videos? +

No, we do not store your downloaded videos. Our process works as follows:

  • Videos are temporarily processed on our servers
  • Files are automatically deleted within 30 minutes
  • We don't keep any permanent copies
  • Your privacy and data security are our priorities
Can I download playlists? +

Currently, our service is designed for individual video downloads. For playlists, you'll need to download each video separately by pasting individual video URLs.

We're considering adding playlist support in the future based on user feedback!
